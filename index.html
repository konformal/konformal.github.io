QUANTUM STARSHIP

HuaXing Zhu is a full professor at the school of physics, Peking University.
His main research interests are quantum field theories for elementary particle physics,
with an emphasis on understanding high-energy scattering at current and future colliders.

朱华星，北大物理学院博雅特聘教授，
追寻量子场论与基本粒子物理之谜，
特别关注现今及未来对撞机的高能散射之理。

Work

I work at the intersection of theories and phenomenologies in elementary particle physics. I am particularly interested in the realization of beautiful mathematical structures in nature.

吾身工于微观粒子之理论与现象之交，
尤其钟爱自然中美丽数学结构之实现。
